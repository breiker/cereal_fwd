template<class Archive>
struct cereal::longdouble< Archive >

Nearest (by precision) floating point type to longdouble supported by Archive.

long double may not be supported by some archives. It's not well defined and it's hard to keep serialized form portable form between platforms. 64bit, 80bit and 128 bit precision can be encountered.
